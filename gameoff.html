The Theme: Moonshot

Mission 13151514 (Mission Moon) is done and its nothing like we had planned.

My partner in crime and love of my life is also my game jam partner. He's the one that makes stuff look cool and tolerates me being bossy. Then I make stuff happen in the background. More specifically, graphic design is done by my partner and both of us are working on it as programmers.

Although we still would like to do some more debugging and updates once the voting is over, some of the things I have immplemented so far:

  • Design documentation
  • A* Pathfinding for enemy AI
  • Enemy AI for closest targets.
  • Enemy combat
  • Particle systems

About the Game

The game was originally to be a real time space survival resource management game but ended up being overhauled
